Kuchiba Chisa – her name itself is a paradox: “withered leaf” and “thousand blossoms”. An 18‑year‑old student carrying the weight of 38 years, a Mutant Resonator who wields the power of Havoc to protect the bonds she holds dear.

Calm, analytical, and unfailingly level‑headed, Chisa believes everything can be understood – except the fragile, unpredictable nature of human connection. It is precisely this fragility, she says, that makes bonds worth fighting for.
